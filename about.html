A Little About Me

🎓 Who am I?

I am a Junior Frontend Developer and a full-time Computer Science student at Politechnika Świętokrzyska in Kielce. I am incredibly passionate about crafting modern, responsive web applications and bridging the gap between beautiful design and flawless functionality. My goal is to always deliver clean, efficient code and perfect the user interface down to the last pixel.

💻 My Tech Stack

In my daily development workflow, I actively build web applications using core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with modern frameworks and tools such as React, Astro, and Vercel for fast production deployments. Beyond the frontend, I have a strong fundamental understanding of how things work under the hood. I actively study low-level computer systems, including C/C++ memory management, x86 architecture, and digital circuit logic.

🏋️‍♂️ Beyond the Screen

When I step away from my code editor, I live a highly active and balanced lifestyle. You can usually find me at the gym pushing through an intense full-body split, playing football, or capturing moments with my camera. I am also deeply interested in culinary arts—I love cooking hearty, high-calorie meals from scratch, from baking fresh apple pies to making the perfect chicken ciabatta sandwich. To wind down, I enjoy competitive gaming like Valorant or fine-tuning setups in Assetto Corsa, and I even dedicate time to cultivating and caring for my indoor orchids.
